Welcome To Our Hotel

Cuban Code Hotel, nestled in the heart of Havana, is a boutique accommodation blending modern comforts with authentic Cuban charm. With vibrant decor inspired by local culture, guests enjoy a range of accommodations, from cozy rooms to luxurious suites. Amenities include a rooftop pool and bar, fitness center, and on-site restaurant serving delectable Cuban cuisine. Perfectly situated for exploring the city's attractions, Cuban Code Hotel offers travelers a memorable and immersive experience in the lively streets of Havana .

Location: Situated in a vibrant neighborhood of Havana, Cuban Code Hotel provides easy access to popular attractions, historical sites, and cultural experiences. Design and Atmosphere: The hotel boasts a distinctive design inspired by Cuba's rich history and artistic heritage. From colorful murals to traditional Cuban architecture, guests are immersed in the local culture from the moment they arrive.
